> 1-norm

$$\| w \|_1 = |w_1| + |w_2| + ... + |w_N| $$ 

> 2-norm

$$\| w \|_2 = (|w_1|^2 + |w_2|^2 + ... + |w_N|^2)^\frac{1}{2} $$ 

> p-norm

$$\| w \|_p = (|w_1|^p + |w_2|^p + ... + |w_N|^p)^\frac{1}{p} $$
